Privacy Policy – CipherThis Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Cipher" developed by Rania Amara .1. Purpose of the Application Cipher is a digital eBook library and reading application that provides users with access to a curated collection of public domain storybooks, focusing on mystery, crime, and adventure novels. The application is designed to offer an immersive reading experience, allowing users to browse classic literature, read books directly within the app, save their progress, and receive reading updates. .2. Information Usage & Core Features To provide a smooth, continuous, and personalized reading experience, the app utilizes the following features:Authentication: Used to create, secure, and synchronize your personalized reading profile, favorites, and history across your devices via Firebase.Reading Progress Tracking: Saving your current page, active chapter, and "continue reading" state so you can instantly resume where you left off. Library Favorites & Analytics: Keeping logs of your reading milestones, bookmarks, finished books, and personalized list of crime, mystery, or adventure novels. Push Notifications: Sending automated reminders, reading suggestions, or system updates to keep you engaged with your library collection. .3. Data Collection & Third-Party Services Cipher uses Google Firebase and native mobile plugins to deliver a reliable, cross-platform experience. Core data is synchronized securely to the cloud to ensure consistency across your authorized devices:Personal & Account Data: We collect your name and email address securely via Firebase Authentication to provision and manage your unique user account profile.Reading Progress & Library History: Your active bookmarks, reading progress timestamps, "continue reading" logs, and reading lists are stored reliably in Google Cloud Firestore.Notification Tokens: Devices tokens are processed securely to route and deliver your custom push notifications. Security & Infrastructure: We do not have access to your personal account passwords. All sensitive authentication traffic and session tokens are isolated and managed securely by Google's dedicated infrastructure. .4. Data Sharing & Third Parties We maintain a strict stance on data privacy. We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal library data, reading habits, or account information to advertisers or unauthorized third-party marketing companies. Your data is handled exclusively by:Google Firebase: For secure data cloud hosting, real-time database state management, push notification routing, and user authentication. Google Play Services / Apple App Store: For native application bundle distribution, update delivery, and baseline performance telemetry. .5. Content & Public Domain License DisclaimerAll books, stories, and novels made available through Cipher belong strictly to the public domain.The application only distributes literature whose copyright protections have legally expired under applicable international copyright laws.Users are free to read, download, and enjoy these classic works of mystery, crime, and adventure without digital rights management (DRM) restrictions. 6. Children's Privacy Cipher operates as a literature utility and reading application. While the public domain content focuses on mystery and adventure stories, the application does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The application complies with all COPPA and GDPR-K standards regarding the protection of minors. .7. Data Deletion Rights Your data and your reading history belong entirely to you. You maintain the right to request the total permanent deletion of your profile account, account credentials, configuration preferences, and all historical reading progress stored within our Firebase databases at any time.8. Data Retention PolicyWe explicitly define our data retention practices as follows:Active Accounts: We retain your personal data, app configurations, and reading progress metadata only for as long as your account remains active and open to maintain your synced reading history.Inactive Accounts: If an account remains completely inactive for a continuous period of 24 months, we reserve the right to automatically and permanently delete all associated user data and library records from our databases. Upon Deletion Request: If you request deletion via email or within the app, your personal information, credentials, and progress records will be permanently deleted from our live Firebase databases within 30 days. Backup copies are automatically overwritten and destroyed within a cycle not exceeding 60 days. .9. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in mobile software functionality, platform architecture updates, notification configurations, or evolving legal frameworks. .10.
